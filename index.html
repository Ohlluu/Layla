About Me

My Artistic Journey

Discover the passion, dedication, and dreams that drive my love for musical theatre

The Beginning

My love for musical theatre began at age 8 when I first saw "Hamilton" on Broadway. The way the performers could make audiences laugh, cry, and feel every emotion through song and story ignited a passion that has only grown stronger with each passing year.

The Journey

As a junior at Lincoln High School, I've been fortunate to perform in numerous productions while training in voice, dance, and acting. Every role teaches me something new about character development, stage presence, and the magic of live performance.

The Dream

I dream of attending a top conservatory program and eventually performing on Broadway while also exploring opportunities in film and television. My goal is to create meaningful art that moves people and tells important stories.
